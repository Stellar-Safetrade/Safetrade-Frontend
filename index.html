How it works

Four steps. Zero trust required.

01
🤝

Agree on terms

Buyer and seller agree on item, price, and deadline. SafeTrade generates a unique trade link.

02
🔒

Buyer locks funds

Buyer deposits XLM or USDC into the Soroban escrow contract. Funds are locked — not held by us.

03
📦

Seller delivers

Seller ships the item or delivers the product. Both parties can track trade status on-chain.

04
✅

Buyer confirms

Buyer confirms receipt. The contract releases funds to the seller instantly on Stellar.
